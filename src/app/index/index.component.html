NIHCTU will provide a versatile clinical research environment enabling the NIH mission to improve human health by:

  • Bringing together diverse stake holders from across the clinical research enterprise and harness their expertise and investments to collaboratively build an open, integrated global research system
  • Integrating information technologies and interoperable standards, policies and practices across the globe
  • Enhancing drug development safety, quality and efficiency worldwide and industry wide to benefit all stake holders especially patients.
